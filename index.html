About Me

TL:DR - I've yet to find a subject I don't enjoy. Stay curious!


I believe following my curiosity will give me the tools to achieve my dreams. I dream of helping people by building the software that does its job so well, it goes unnoticed. I dream of being part of a team with a passion for coding and a passion for solving difficult problems and a passion for ceaseless learning. I dream of allowing others to do the same by contributing to and building intuitive and effective learning tools, whether it be by creating APIs that access free resources, or optimizing tools already in existence, I want to be part of that field. I dream of what this driving curiosity may bring; What story has it already spun in my life, and what is the next chapter?
